The Sun is the star at the center of our solar system. Here are some facts:

  • Circumference: 4.37 million kilometers
  • Distance from Earth: 149.6 million kilometers
  • Maximum Temperature: 15 million degrees Celsius
  • Minimum Temperature: 3,500 degrees Celsius (sunspots)
